TON timeline
Late 2018

The Durov brothers, founders of Telegram Messenger, release the first version of the Telegram Open Network Whitepaper.

2020

Telegram was forced to cease active development of TON.A vibrant open-source community contonues to maintain the network an build new TON functionality.bringing Telegram s design to fruition.

2022

TON becomes The Open Network.TON nainnet is fully live operational, hosting ultra-cheap, fast and ecologically friendly transactions.
